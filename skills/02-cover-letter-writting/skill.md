## UNIVERSAL RULES (ALL FORMATS)

### Structure
- **Length:** 250-400 words (3/4 page max). Never exceed 1 page.
- **Paragraphs:** 3-4 paragraphs. No paragraph longer than 4 sentences.
- **Font:** Match the resume font. 10.5-12pt.
- **File:** PDF only. Filename: `FirstName_LastName_CoverLetter_Company.pdf`

### Content Rules
1. **Opening line must hook** â€” No "I am writing to apply for..."
2. **Company-specific in paragraph 1** â€” Prove you researched them
3. **2-3 achievements with metrics** â€” Not duties, achievements
4. **Mirror the JD language** â€” Use their exact keywords
5. **Close with confidence, not desperation** â€” "I look forward to 
   discussing" not "I hope you'll consider"
6. **Never repeat the resume** â€” Expand on 2-3 highlights only

### What to NEVER Include
- Salary expectations (unless explicitly asked)
- Reasons for leaving current job
- Apologies for gaps or lack of experience
- Generic statements that could apply to any company
- "References available upon request"
