## UNIVERSAL RULES (ALL FORMATS)

### Terminology
- **Resume** = 1-2 pages, used in US, Canada, Australia (most private sector)
- **CV** = 2+ pages, used in EU, Asia, academia, medicine, international orgs
- Always ask/detect which the user needs based on target region

### Formatting Rules
- **Font:** Calibri, Arial, Garamond, or Cambria. 10-12pt body, 14-16pt name.
- **Margins:** 0.5"-1" all sides
- **File format:** PDF (unless employer specifies .docx)
- **Filename:** `FirstName_LastName_Resume_TargetCompany.pdf`
- **NO:** Tables, text boxes, headers/footers (ATS can't read them), 
  graphics, photos (except Asia/EU where required), columns 
  (single-column is safest for ATS)
- **YES:** Standard section headers (ATS looks for exact words):
  "Experience" "Education" "Skills" "Summary" "Certifications"

### Content Rules
1. **Every bullet = Achievement, not duty**
   - âŒ "Responsible for managing team"
   - âœ… "Led 12-person engineering team; shipped 4 releases on time, 
     reducing bugs 35%"
2. **Use the X-Y-Z Formula (Google's):**
   "Accomplished [X] as measured by [Y], by doing [Z]"
3. **Quantify EVERYTHING possible:**
   Revenue, %, team size, budget, users, time saved, # of projects
4. **Mirror job description keywords** â€” exact phrases
5. **Most recent experience first** â€” reverse chronological
6. **Remove anything older than 10-15 years** (unless exceptional)
7. **No personal pronouns** â€” No "I", "me", "my"
8. **Start every bullet with a power verb** (see power-verbs.md)
