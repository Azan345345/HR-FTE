## ğŸ‡ºğŸ‡¸ UNITED STATES RULES

### Resume
- **Length:** 1 page (0-10 YOE), 2 pages max (10+ YOE)
- **Photo:** NEVER (illegal to consider in hiring)
- **DOB/Age:** NEVER
- **Gender:** NEVER
- **Marital status:** NEVER
- **Nationality:** NEVER (only work authorization: 
  "Authorized to work in the US" or "US Citizen")
- **Format:** Reverse chronological (dominant)
- **GPA:** Include if >3.5 AND within 3 years of graduation
- **Address:** City, State only (no street address)
- **References:** Never on resume; never "available upon request"

### Cover Letter
- Tone: Confident, achievement-oriented, direct
- First name basis acceptable after first interaction
- Personality and culture-fit signals are valued
- Story-telling approach works well
- "I" is acceptable (unlike resume)

### Email
- Informal-professional tone
- First names OK immediately in tech/startup
- Keep under 150 words
- Follow up after 5 business days

### Key Cultural Notes
- Emphasize individual achievements
- "Self-starter," "ownership," "impact" are valued words
- Diversity & inclusion awareness matters
- Always include LinkedIn URL
